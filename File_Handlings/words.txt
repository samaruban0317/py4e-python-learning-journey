Lines - Math Steps, Examples & QuestionsA line is a fundamental
 one-dimensional geometric figure that is perfectly straight,
 has infinite length but no width or thickness, and extends forever
 in both directions, made up of countless points. Lines form the basis 
of geometry and can be horizontal, vertical, parallel, perpendicular,
 or intersecting, serving as building blocks for shapes and graphs in
 mathematics and real-world applications like engineering.
